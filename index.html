BIO

Kelly Morris grew up in rural Camden, South Carolina. He picked up the guitar at age 6 and began teaching himself how to read music. Genres of Soul, Blues, and Jazz music were very much a part of Kelly's upbringing and focus of study. At 23, Kelly would find himself writing, audio engineering, and self-producing for him and his brother’s band, “The Mobros.” Starting in 2014, every year has consisted of major US tours (playing 250+ shows a year) opening for acts like BB King, Band of Horses, Rainbow Kitten Surprise, The Wood Brothers, and Houndmouth. “It took a learning curve and consistent strategizing to manage ourselves without a label. Honestly the fun overshadowed the work that was required.” This year, Kelly has found himslef in a very similar situation with a new learning curve, only now with coding. "It has been very challenging. Learning Javascript, CSS, and HTML has been like learning several new languages at once that compliment and yet embrace eachother. Much like audio engineering, the fun is still overshadowing the work."
